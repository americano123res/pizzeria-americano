Our Story

Welcome to Pizzeria Americano

Nestled in the heart of beautiful Ohrid, on Branko Shotra 2, Pizzeria Americano has been delighting locals and visitors with authentic Italian flavors since 1996. Our handcrafted pizzas, fresh pastas, and warm hospitality make every meal a memorable experience.

Whether you're exploring the UNESCO-listed old town, relaxing by stunning Lake Ohrid, or simply craving the perfect pizza — we're here to serve you with love and tradition.
